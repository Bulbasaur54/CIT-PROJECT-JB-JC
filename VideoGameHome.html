Video Game Systems

Video Game systems have transformed over the years greatly.
The first known game system that was released is called the b-
rown box. It was developed by a small team that was headed
by Ralph H. Baer. They worked for a company known as Sanders
Associates who then released the console under Magnavox.
This occured in September of 1972 and was sold abroad a year
later.
